ZOMATO DRONE SERVICES

Zomato is one of the most booming food delivery services in India. It has been very effective and has never failed in delivering on time. But from recent experiences, the orders received are on time, but not correct. If the order received is wrong, being on time is of no use. To overcome this problem of misplacing the orders, the implementation of the drones would be a better alternative.

Working:

1. Once the order is booked, the user is given an OTP.
2. After the order is prepared, it is put into a compartment of the drone.
3. The drone reaches the location and sends the pictures of the surroundings so that the user can identify the exact location of the drone.
4. Once the drone is located, the OTP is to be entered.
5. Once it is entered, the compartment of the drone in which the food is placed is opened and the user can take his food.

Advantages:

1. The number of orders the drone can carry is more than that of any person being able it without being confused.
2. Faster and more efficient than traditional delivery.
3. Long-distance orders can also be made.

Disadvantages:

1. Flying a drone requires many permissions.
2. Expensive.
